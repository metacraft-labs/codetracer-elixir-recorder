  - expected exit code: =0=

  DERIVATION: The flood fixture spawns one child and uses three message
  classes:
  1. The child sends =flush_child_ready= once before entering the loop.
  2. The parent sends 64 =flush_ping= messages (=lists:foreach/2= with

  3. After consuming all =flush_ping= messages in order (the guard
     =Index =:= Seen + 1= enforces sequential delivery), the child sends
     =flush_done= once and exits.

  The deterministic loop ensures send/receive pairs are bijective and
  ordered, regardless of scheduler nondeterminism.
